Sonic Happy Hour - Menu, Time & Schedule 2025

What is Sonic Happy Hour?

If you’re someone who enjoys grabbing a quick drink or snack without spending too much, then the Sonic Happy Hour is made for you. During this special time, you can enjoy half-price drinks, refreshing slushes, and other favorites from the Sonic menu.

What makes it even better is how simple it is — you just pull up, place your order, and enjoy your favorites for less. The Sonic Happy Hour menu changes from time to time, with new flavors and limited-time deals that keep things exciting.

Sonic Happy Hour Time

Sonic Happy Hour is your go-to time for big drink savings on the Sonic menu. It’s a short daily window when many drinks and slushes drop to about half price.

Regular Happy Hour Time

Most Sonic locations run their Happy Hour from 2 PM to 4 PM. Some spots even offer late-night Happy Hours for those evening cravings.

Sonic’s Late-Night Happy Hour

The Sonic Happy Hour menu keeps the fun going late into the night, usually starting around 8 PM and lasting until closing time.
